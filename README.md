# Climate Story AI

> Turning climate science into stories that inspire understanding and action.

---

## Overview

Climate Story AI is an AI-powered knowledge communication system designed to transform complex climate data, scientific research, and environmental information into accessible stories.

The system bridges the gap between climate science and public understanding by helping people connect data with real-world impacts.

Instead of presenting climate information only through numbers and reports, Climate Story AI creates human-centered narratives that make climate challenges easier to understand.

---

## The Problem

Climate change is one of the most important challenges facing humanity.

However, climate knowledge is often difficult for the public to engage with because:

- Scientific information is complex
- Data is difficult to interpret
- Reports often lack human context
- People struggle to connect global trends with daily life

Better communication is essential to transform scientific understanding into meaningful action.
